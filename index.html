ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Méjeane Simoné Claassen. I believe I was created to create. I started with Interior Decorating al the way through to being a Graphic and now, Web designer. I love working with colour and I use it as a skill to make my work pop! I enjoy the developing side of a website, but I focus more on the design. I am always ready for a challenge, which I feel is necessary in this industry. Design aside, I am loyal, hilarious and absolutely insist on enjoying life!
